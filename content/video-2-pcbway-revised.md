# Video 2 — Custom PCB Revised Build

## Working title

**My Breadboard Was Ugly, So I Designed a Telegraph PCB**

## Core promise

Turn the messy Arduino prototype into a custom-shaped printed circuit board.

## Build scope

- Custom telegraph/telegram PCB shape
- A-Z LED Morse chart
- Big key button
- Mode / Mute / Rec-Play buttons
- Signal LED
- Buzzer
- Shift registers or LED driver
- Secret URL silkscreen
- Board order and unboxing
- 3D printed backer or stand

## Video structure

1. Show breadboard mess.
2. Explain what needs improvement.
3. Design schematic.
4. Design custom PCB outline.
5. Add labels and Easter eggs.
6. Export Gerbers.
7. Order prototype boards.
8. Solder and test board.
9. Demo secret triggers.
10. Tease smart decoder version.
